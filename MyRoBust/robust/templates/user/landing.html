#1 What is RoBust for?

RoBust a newly innovated bus reservation system for Technologians, It is a web application that provides real user convenience in purchasing bus tickets. CIT – U passengers can select and reserve a bus seat by themselves through the system's seat selector. Generally, this system allows Technologians to experience a convenient bus seat reservation ahead of time. Another goal of this system is to reduce the conventional paper ticketing method and to provide a new way for bus ticketing in the IMDC Office. Wildcats Bus indeed provides comfort and safety for all Technologians when going home especially during rush hours. With RoBust system, this adds value to CIT – U passengers’ satisfaction in terms of their convenience.

#2 How does it works?

The system has features that allows users to login and logout of their account. This allows the passengers to check the latest update on scheduled time departure of the buses, trip details and available seats in a particular time and date. Confirmed bookings will be redirected to the Booking Review where the user can view or reschedule the booking prior to departure time. Furthermore, it prompts the machine to display the electronic ticket of the passenger, showing ticket details that will act as proof of reservation. All reservations are counted and shown on the dashboard, where users can view the overview of the analytics of the booking history and the number of purchased tickets. Each consumer has an electronic wallet that serves this system's payment method and this can be loaded at the IMDC Office by purchasing a load. This system would use artificial intelligence, a voice command that allows easier and quicker to access in provide general customer service needs and experience.

#3 What are the advanteges in using RoBust?

It is a realtime and easy process in which it takes two or less than a minute to reserve a certain product/item anytime and anywhere. In addition, some systems would link payment channels to allow users to instantly pay the reservation with less worries. There will be no more longer queues, since users can manage to reserve on their own.
